Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layered flat pasta, meat or vegetables, cheese, and a tomato-based sauce. The pasta sheets are alternated with fillings, creating a layered casserole that is baked in the oven. The most common filling for lasagna is a meat-based sauce made with beef, pork, or a combination of both, mixed with a tomato-based sauce and spices. The dish is topped with mozzarella, parmesan, or other grated cheese that melts and forms a golden crust during baking. Some variations of lasagna also include ricotta cheese as a filling, adding creaminess to the dish.

Lasagna is traditionally served in square portions, with a hearty sauce and tender noodles that are both filling and flavorful. It is often served with a green salad or garlic bread and is a popular comfort food for many families and a staple dish at celebrations and events.

Lasagna Ingredients

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
